      Rhythm Spikes?

      The rhythm spike counter is incremented when:

      it is not the first keypress, the current expected character is not a space, the previous accepted character was not a space, and the time since the previous keypress is over 300ms. TLDR; it means the time between key presses is long. Each spike subtracts 1.2 from the total score up to a max of -20 points.
